Core Features

Essential tools that make MediTrack your trusted medication companion.

Medication Management

Manage your medications easily: add, edit, and track dosage, frequency, and schedules.

Reminder Notifications

Automated notifications to remind you of scheduled medication times and important updates.

Health Logging

Log your health data such as mood, side effects, and vitals to track progress.

Shared Access

Share your health data with caregivers or family members for monitoring and support.

Exporting History

Download or share your health and medication history as a PDF for easy reference.
