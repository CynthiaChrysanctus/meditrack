Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How does MediTrack work?

MediTrack helps you track your medication schedule by sending you reminders and keeping a record of your progress.

Can I set reminders for multiple medications?

Yes, you can set reminders for each medication, with options for time, dosage, and frequency.

Is the MediTrack app available on both Android and iOS?

Yes, MediTrack is available for download on both the App Store and Google Play Store.

How do I reset my password?
